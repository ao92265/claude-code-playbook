# Top 10 Claude Code Questions Businesses Are Asking Right Now

**Source:** Reza Rezvani, cited in Medium Daily Digest of 2026-04-07 (Email 23). 15 min read, 106 claps.

## Key takeaways

- **Three recurring clusters** surfacing from Rezvani's customer conversations: automation, productivity, compliance.
- Useful as sales/evangelism reference when engineering leadership at Harris is pitching Claude Code adoption to business stakeholders.
- Companion to Rezvani's [other April 2026 pieces]({{ site.baseurl }}/docs/news/cli-vs-mcp/) — same author, same lens, focused on business stakeholder framing.

## Why this matters

Technical engineers assume business stakeholders ask technical questions. They don't. The three clusters in Rezvani's article are what **CEOs, COOs, and CFOs** actually ask:

1. **Automation:** "What can we automate? How much time does it save? What's the ROI?"
2. **Productivity:** "Will my engineers be faster? How do we measure it?"
3. **Compliance:** "What happens with our code/data? How do we audit this?"

Engineering leadership needs answers to all three, not just #2.
