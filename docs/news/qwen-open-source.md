# Qwen Just Quietly Became the Most Dangerous Open-Source AI Model

**Source:** Suleiman Tawil, cited in Medium Daily Digest of 2026-04-07 (Email 23). 7 min read, 419 claps.

## Key takeaways

- **Qwen is now the most-downloaded AI model family on Earth.**
- Built by Chinese AI lab; open weights; quietly outpacing Western alternatives by adoption volume.
- Part of the broader shift: open-source models (Qwen, Gemma 4) are catching up to closed-source models for specific use cases.

## Why it matters for Harris

- **Gemma 4** (April 2026 release) gets a lot of attention for being Apache 2.0 — but **Qwen has been eating market share quietly** for months.
- Evaluation point: if Harris considers open-weights alternatives for regulated customers, Qwen and Gemma 4 are both on the table.
- Regulatory consideration: Chinese AI model may face different compliance questions than Western-origin models in some verticals (public sector, defence, certain financial scenarios).
