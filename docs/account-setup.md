# Claude Account Setup

How to create your Anthropic account and choose the right plan before installing Claude Code.

---

## Sign Up

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. Click **Sign up** and register with your email address (or Google/SSO if available)
3. Verify your email and complete onboarding

That's it — you now have a Claude account with Free tier access.

---

## Plan Tiers

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|:-----|:------|:---------|
| **Free** | $0 | Trying Claude out — limited messages per day |
| **Pro** | $20/month | Regular use — higher message limits, priority access |
| **Max** | $100/month or $200/month | Heavy daily use, Claude Code, extended thinking, highest limits |
| **Team** | $30/user/month | Organisations — shared billing, admin controls, team management |

### Which plan should you pick?

- **Free** — Fine for evaluation. You'll hit message limits quickly during real work.
- **Pro** — Good for occasional Claude Code use or web-only workflows.
- **Max** — **Recommended for Claude Code users.** The higher token limits and extended thinking make a real difference during multi-file sessions, debugging, and multi-agent workflows. The $200/month tier adds even higher limits for power users.
- **Team** — The right choice when rolling out Claude Code across a team. Centralised billing and admin controls simplify management.

> **Note:** Prices shown are as of early 2025 and may change. Always check [claude.ai/pricing](https://claude.ai/pricing) for current figures.

---

## After Signing Up

Once you have an active plan:

1. **Install Claude Code** — See [Getting Started](getting-started.md) or [Step 1: Installation](../onboarding/01-install.md)
2. **Authenticate** — Run `claude` in your terminal and follow the login prompt
3. **Verify** — Run `claude --version` to confirm everything works
